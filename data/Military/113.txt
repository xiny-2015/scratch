    In new network on 29 April, according to the international online report, the International Atomic Energy Agency Director General Elbaradei 28 to the UN Security Council on the Iran nuclear issue report, the report said Iran did not comply with Security Council presidential statement to suspend its nuclear activities.
    Elbaradei said in the report, Iran is not in the period of 30 days of the provisions of the United Nations Security Council presidential statement in suspend nuclear activities, but continue to enrich uranium. The report also noted the recent period of time, Iran has accelerated its uranium enrichment activities, and successfully extracted the enriched uranium.
    The UN Security Council will hold an informal meeting in May 3rd, to discuss the Elbaradei report. (Mo Zhiwei)
