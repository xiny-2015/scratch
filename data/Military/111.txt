    British "Jane's Defence Weekly" in 2006 12 April issue reports Pakistan officially ready to accept a total of 9 aircraft P-3C
    "Orion" maritime patrol aircraft (MPA), USA Naval Air Systems Command will then the contract awarded to Lockhead Martin company. The initial contract worth $6000000, mainly for the upgrading of the first two P-3C aircraft. Pakistan P-3C aircraft will be ready for delivery by non upgrade America Navy standard, adopt the new wing and replacement of many spare parts, but also the task of upgrading the system. In addition, will also provide technical maintenance, all 9 aircraft contract worth about $30000000, the plane after the upgrade the service life will be extended to 15000 hours.
