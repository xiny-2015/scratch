    Zhang Jianqi, Huang Ping in the ceremony
    Qianlong military reports: according to the China State Shipbuilding Corporation website news, April 18th, new surveying ship (shipyard built code H2376) in Jiangnan Shipyard (Group) Corporation shipbuilding division started processing workshop, deputy director of the general armaments department Zhang Jianqi chiefs attended the ceremony, Jiangnan Shipyard (Group) Corporation Vice Chairman, Party Secretary Huang Ping presided over the ceremony. The ship group deputy chief engineer Chen Jinhai attended.
