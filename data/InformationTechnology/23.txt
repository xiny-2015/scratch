Foreign news HP released five re design notebook products on Tuesday.
It is reported, in which five products in two paragraph mainly for entertainment applications, while the HP DV 2000 will be the company's first support AMD Turion X2 processor notebook products.
In addition, HP also said, the company will join the wireless operator Vodafone, continue to promote "notebook embedded wide area network chip" technology, and revealed the future launch notebook products will add to Vodafone HSDPA network support.
