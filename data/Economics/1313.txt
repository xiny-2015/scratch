Xinhuanet.com Beijing on 9 May, May 7th to 9, held in China for the Fifth China Japan strategic dialogue Vice Foreign Minister Dai Bingguo with Japan's Foreign Affairs Minister Yamauchi Masataro.
The two sides on major issues of common concern in the current Sino Japanese relations in-depth exchange of views. Dai Bingguo comprehensively expounded the President Hu Jintao met in March 31st with Japan China Friendship seven team spirit responsible person's speech, I hope our joint efforts, the Japanese side especially to the opposite action, to remove political obstacles affecting the improvement and development of bilateral relations as soon as possible..
The two sides agree, will be held the fifth round of consultations on the East China Sea issue in mid May. The two sides discussed the possibility of Sino Japanese Foreign Minister meetings on multilateral occasions. (end)
