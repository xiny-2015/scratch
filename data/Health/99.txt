The State Food and Drug Administration recently made the decision, the revocation of polyacrylamide hydrogel (for injection) registration certificate for medical device, from now on full stop the production, sale and use of.
Polyacrylamide hydrogel (for injection) is a kind of soft tissue filling agent, mainly used for breast augmentation, long temporal, zygomatic, cheek, long long long hips and body soft tissue filling and blocking etc.. After investigation, the product can not guarantee the safety of listed in use. The State Food and Drug Administration requirements of production enterprises to immediately cease production and sales of polyacrylamide hydrogel (for injection), to recover all the products have been sold, and destroy the recovery and the inventory of products in the supervision of food and drug supervision and Administration Bureau of the province, do a good job in the treatment of adverse events arising from the use of the company's products.
