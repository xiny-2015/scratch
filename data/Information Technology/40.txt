This Tuesday the South Korean Ministry of information industry revealed that, in the past April, the total amount of native IT exports, more than the previous month for a 13.6% decline, it is reported that price declines and the won exchange rate fluctuations, is the main reason for the above results. According to statistics, in April the month of South Korea IT exports amounted to $8080000000, while imports of IT products than the previous month fell nine percentage points, reaching $4720000000.
